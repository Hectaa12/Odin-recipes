Pupusas

Description

This is the most delicious food to come from Honduran cuisine, although not originally part of Honduran Culture. By some unknown means, this became a dish associated, in one way or another, with Honduran culture and cuisine. By the description found on Google, "a pupusa is a thick griddle cake or flatbread from El Salvador and Honduras made with cornmeal or rice flour."

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal or Rice floour
    • Beans
  • Chicharron or other meat of choice
  • Cheese

    • For the topping:

  • Cabbage
  • Onions
  • vinegar

Steps

  1. First, you're gonna need to locate a vendor of pupusas, Salvadorean or Honduran decent preferably,
  2. Next, you're going to want to understand your taste preferences and what you'd really like to have on in a pupusa.
  3. Finally, you're going to order said items from said vendor.
  4. This step is completely optional. You're going to want to purchase a banana soda from said vendor. If none are available, leave the establishment immediately and seek Honduran assistance.
