Platano Rellenos

Description

This is one of the most filling and satisfying dishes I've ever had in my whole life. It is a fried whole plantain, cut down the middle length-wise, filled with ground beef, tomato sauce, lettuce, cheese, and a bit of sour cream.

Ingredients

  • Plantains
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. First, poke holes with a fork into the plantains before you fry them in a pan on medium high heat.
  2. Then, cook some ground beef with seasonings and added veggies for flavor.
  3. Cut cook plantain through middle to make a divet where toppings will go.
  4. Add ground beef, tomato sauce, lettuce, and then cheese.
