Baleadas

Description

Another staple straight from Honduras. Composed of a flour tortilla, filled with mashed beans, scrambled eggs, thick cream, and crumbled hard salty cheese

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortillas
  • Eggs
  • Beans
  • Sour Cream
  • Crumbly cheese

Steps

  1. First lightly heat the tortillas.
  2. Spread beans onto tortilla.
  3. Add scrambled eggs
  4. Finish with crumbly cheese, and sour cream inside or on top
  5. Toast tortilla with ingredients in pan, folding the tortilla in half to retain its shape.
